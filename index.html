Nash equilibrium
Read more

Nash equilibrium is justified as a reasonable prediction of the outcome. If agreed beforehand on this set of actions - nobody has an incentive to deviate. There may either exist no, 1 or multiple Nash equilibria.

Serial dictatorship
Read more

Serial dictatorship is a simple matching mechanism that produces pareto-efficient and strategy-proof outcome by assigning each person a priority (perhaps randomly). Each pick items in order of their priority.

House allocation TTC
Read more

House allocation TTC is a mechanism that assigns houses to agents based on their preferences. It is strategy-proof, pareto-efficient and respects individual rationality.
